Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries,
and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Featured in:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast

  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

    For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced

  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top

  • 10 ounces brie, cut cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all
    the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorder. You don't nedd to actually knead the
    dough, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when miing by hand to incorporate
    the last of the flour...
